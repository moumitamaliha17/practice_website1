This website is about interesting flowers

I am not the biggest fan of flowers, but it doesn't take a fan to appreciate their beauty does it? In this website, we will learn about some interesting flowers that have more to them than just beauty!

Corpse Flower

Scientific Name: Amorphophallus Titanum
Color: Dark red and green
Native Habitat: Tropical rainforests of Sumatra, Indonesia

These flowers are massive in size and have a foul odor. The smell they emit is similar to that of rotting flesh and they do so to attract pollinators such as carrion beetles and flies.

Venus Flytrap

Scientific Name: Dionaea Muscipula
Color: Red and green
Native Habitat: Wetlands of North and South Carolina

These plants are carnivorous! They swiftly shut their traps up to catch prey and then feed on their nutrients which the plants' soil lacked.
